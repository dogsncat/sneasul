29th july 2022 (2) - Unfinished, old, and traditional art dump

This is an art dump of all of the old/alt pieces of art I have on my hard drives. Some of this is stuff I think is good—and I'll comment a bit on pieces I think were interesting to me as I was looking through them—while others are still quite embarrasing to share, but nonetheless they're here on this page!

I hope you enjoy looking at some of these. I've always enjoyed artists releasing their scraps, so hopefully you find this interesting too!

Oh and also, they're not in any chronological order or anything. I'm not sure I have exact enough dates to do that. They're in order of how interesting/good they are (in my opinion).

I'm actually very proud of this one - it was done it Photoshop on a whim when my friend was encouraging me to make more art. A period in my life when I was completely burned out by an art course I did. 2018 probably.

These were done in a software called Rebelle 4. Very powerful simulation software for stuff like realistic watercolours, oil paint, and other mediums. I could never get it to look as good as the real stuff haha, but if you are a much better artist than I am then you'd be able to get some killer stuff out of it. Here's a link to the software.
