Terms of Service

1. Payment

Payment will be made via PayPal invoicing.

The invoice will need to be paid in full upon reciept before the Artist can start work on the commission.

Any delays in paying the invoice may result in the commission being cancelled. The terms outlined in (3) will not apply and the Client will be fully refunded.

2. Cancellation

If the Client does not respond to any communication (email or direct messaging) within 5 days the commission will be cancelled and the Client will be refunded according to the terms outlined in (3).

The Artist reserves the right to cancel a commission if the Client is repeatedly uncooperative or harasses the Artist. The Client will be refunded according to the terms outlined in (3).

If the Artist cancels the commission for any other reason not covered in the terms above, the terms outlined in (3) will not apply and the Client will be fully refunded.

If the Client cancels the commission for any reason, the Client will be refunded according to the terms outlined in (3).

3. Refunds

A commission will be fully refunded up until the point the Artist finalises the sketch with the Client.

After the Artist finalises the sketch with the Client, a 40% cancellation fee will be applied to the price of the commission and taken off the refund amount. (For example, the Client would be refunded £30 if the commission is £50.)

4. Revisions

A reasonable amount of revisions can be made at any point during the commission.

The Artist may charge extra for any major revisions which drastically alters the piece. This charge is at the Artist's discretion and will be agreed upon with the Client.

5. Rights

The Artist retains all copyright.
