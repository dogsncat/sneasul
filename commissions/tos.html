Important terms to avoid conflict have been highlighted blue.

1. Payment

Payment will be made via PayPal invoicing.

The invoice will need to be paid in full upon reciept before I can start work on the commission.

Any delays in paying the invoice may result in the commission being cancelled. The terms outlined in (3) will not apply and you will be fully refunded.

2. Cancellation

If you do not respond to any communication (email or direct messaging) within 5 days the commission will be cancelled and you will be refunded according to the terms outlined in (3).

I reserve the right to cancel a commission if you are repeatedly uncooperative or harass me. You will be refunded according to the terms outlined in (3).

If I cancel the commission for any other reason not covered in the terms above, the terms outlined in (3) will not apply and you will be fully refunded.

3. Refunds

A commission will be fully refunded up until the point I start colouring.

After I have started colouring, a 40% cancellation fee will be applied to the price of the commission and taken off the refund amount. (You would be refunded £30 if the commission is £50.)

4. Revisions

A reasonable amount of minor or major revisions can be made in the sketch stage of the commission.

Any major revisions that drastically alters the composition, poses, or background after the sketch stage will incur extra charges. This charge will be 50% of the commission price for every new revision. (You will be charged £25 per revision if the commission is £50.)
